## Supported Sources

Robertify supports a variety of sources. These include:
- Spotify
- Apple Music
- Deezer
- SoundCloud
- Vimeo
- Twitch
- Bandcamp
- Beam
- Getyarn
- HTTP Audio Streams
- Local Audio Files (MP3, MP4, OGG, WAV, FLAC,...)

You can play tracks, artists, albums, playlists and livestreams from these sources.
